Score File Formatting

Our score files align closely with the Step 5 score, with the only modifications being the addition of pitch adjustments, effects-related properties, and piano-specific attributes.

Drum Score

Drums play both the wav samples and the synthesized drum:

<instrument instrument="DrumInstrument">
    <note measure="1" beat="1" duration="1" note="mpccyrid" pitch="3"/>
    <note measure="1" beat="2" duration="1" note="synthdrum_1" pitch="1"/>
</instrument>

Effects Score

Effects are functional for both Drums and Piano during testing. Adjust the threshold, wet, and dry levels for a more distinct sound:

<instrument instrument="NoiseGate">
    <note measure="1" delay=".0025" wet="1" dry="0" threshold=".1" />
    <note measure="2" delay=".0025" wet=".75" dry=".25" threshold=".1" />
    <note measure="2" delay=".0025" wet=".5" dry=".5" threshold=".1" />
</instrument>
